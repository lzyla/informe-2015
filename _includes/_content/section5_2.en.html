ABC

DigoDiego (Politwoops) was a project that allowed to check if a member of the Congress took back and erased tweeted statements. The project was active until late July 2015. After nineteen months in operation, we decided to shut it down because there were few interesting tweets deleted by members of the Congress, we needed to allocate time to more priority projects and the new terms and conditions of Twitter's API brought uncertainty over the viability of this project. We explain our reasons and the lessons learned during the process here.

Objective as foreseen on the 2015 Action Plan:

Update, upkeep and consolidation of the project
